Navigation

WASD or edge-scroll to move the camera. Hold MMB or Space + drag to pan. Use the mouse wheel to zoom.

Construction

Press 0 for Inspect mode or choose a structure with 1–9. Left-click to place. Drag with Belt to route logistics, or press X and drag to dismantle an area.

Orientation

Press R to rotate the placement direction. Existing structures can be selected and rotated from the inspector.

Production

Miners extract ore or coal. Furnaces consume coal to smelt ore. Assemblers turn plates into components and research packs.

Logistics

Belts carry multiple spaced items and follow visible corners. Every machine outputs in its arrow direction. Routers alternate output between forward and right.

Economy

Send products into the Command Core to earn credits. Research packs advance the operation and unlock milestone rewards.
